Pokémon Battle Game

Game Rules

1. Each player selects a Pokémon.

2. Players take turns choosing an action.

3. Actions:

  • Fight: Deal damage to the opponent.
  • Bag: Use an item (currently not implemented).
  • Run: Attempt to escape the battle (not implemented).
  • Jump: Avoid the next attack (not implemented).

4. Each Pokémon starts with 100 health points (HP).

5. The game lasts for 3 rounds, and the player with the most remaining health at the end wins.

6. Moves are tracked for each player and displayed on their side.
