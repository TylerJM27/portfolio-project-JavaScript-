Roll the Dice Game

Rules:

  • When you click the Roll Dice! button, you will roll up to 10 dice, one at a time.
  • If you roll a 1, the game stops and all further rolls are forfeited.
  • Prime numbers are lucky! If you roll a 2, 3, or 5, you will win that many points and get to roll again.
  • If you roll the non-prime numbers of 4 or 6, you win nothing but you get to roll again.
